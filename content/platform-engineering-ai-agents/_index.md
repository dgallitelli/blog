71% of CDOs are experimenting with generative AI. Only 6% have it in production. This series examines why the gap exists and how platform engineering closes it.

**[Part 1: Why Your AI Program Stalls Between Pilot and Production](part1-the-production-gap/)** — The business case. The 12:1 ratio between experimentation and production, the productivity illusion, and five diagnostic questions for your next AI strategy review.

**[Part 2: The Platform Engineering Playbook](part2-the-platform-engineering-playbook/)** — The technical architecture. The "agents on / agents in" framework, the vendor landscape, governance requirements, and the autonomy ladder.

**[Part 3: The AWS Playbook — From AgentCore to Agent Registry](part3-the-aws-playbook/)** — The implementation. How Amazon Bedrock AgentCore maps to the architecture, a Thomson Reuters case study, the new Agent Registry, and a maturity-based adoption path.
